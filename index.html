Our Story

Two friends, one code.

We're Jacob and Patrick — friends since middle school, through every workout, every job, and the long stretch of years where life gets heavy and you find out who's still standing beside you.

What we're building here is bigger than anything we'll ever hand you: the whole idea behind the name. The mindset, the miles, the long game, and the simple belief that ordinary people doing the hard thing right is worth it.

Because somewhere in the miles, we figured out the rest of it: life needs a playbook, a system, a mindset. Today is yesterday's tomorrow. So get after it.

The Creed

You already know the feeling. The alarm nobody set. The work that calls when no one's keeping score. The word you keep whether anyone's watching or not.

That's not a mood. It's a decision.

To walk the long road, with purpose. To keep showing up. To take the road less traveled for the challenge it holds. The easy path never paid.

You build yourself by hand — through sweat, blood, and tears, for as long as it takes. Not a phase. Not a program. A lifestyle.

We call it SHEH.
